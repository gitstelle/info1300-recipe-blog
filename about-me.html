你好 (Hello)!

My name is Estelle and I'm a Taiwanese-American college student at Cornell. I grew up eating my dad's delicious home-cooked meals. When I got to college, I realized how spoiled I was by his cooking and that I didn't know how to make any of my favorite comfort foods! I started this blog to document what I've learned so far and and what I will learn in the future!

Thanks for stopping by!
