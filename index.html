Welcome (歡迎)!

还好 (hái hǎo) in Mandarin translates to "fine", "okay", or "good enough" and is pronounced "hi how." Whether your cooking is 还好, 很好 (hěn hǎo = very good), or 不好 (bù hǎo = not good), hi! I'd love to share how to cook my favorite dishes from my Taiwanese-American home.

— Estelle
